Who We Are

Built around horses
and the sport we love

Solera Polo is a boutique polo service founded by Luis and Polina, two professional players who share a life built around horses and the sport they love.

Together, we are more than players for hire. We are a team that understands the sport from every angle: breeding, veterinary care, training, competition, coaching, and the logistics of making it all work seamlessly.

Based between Europe and Argentina, we offer a complete polo experience. Our string of 20 horses travels with us through the European season, selected from Cría Solera and from trusted Argentine bloodlines.

Polo has a reputation for being intimidating. We think it should feel like coming home.

Luis
4-Goal AAP · 3-Goal HPA · Equine Veterinarian

A rare combination of professional player and equine vet. Every horse in our string is selected, trained, and cared for with both a player's instinct and a clinician's eye. Creator of Cría Solera. Spanish and Argentine nationality.

Polina
5-Goal Ladies · 1-Goal Mixed · Mathematician

Professional player with a background in mathematics and corporate partnerships. Structure, precision, and a perspective shaped by worlds beyond the polo field. Hungarian and Russian nationality. Six languages between us.
